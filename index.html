About Me

Hi, I am Divya. I am a software developer, originally from Delhi, India. In December 2015, I graduated from North Carolina State University with an M.S in Computer Science. Before that, I worked as a Senior Mobile Developer for Samsung Research India for more than 2 years, where I developed mobile applications for Samsung Os - Tizen from scratch. Thats when I realized that I had a passion for developing user friendly and highly effective applications to solve complex problems. I am currently looking for a position where I can explore such problems and come up with critical and creative solutions.

While my forte is in Android, I am a quick learner & am proud of my ability to adapt swiftly to languages or frameworks best suited for solving a particular problem. This is demonstrated by project examples like I developed & deployed a Ruby on Rails based library system in 2 weeks without any prior knowledge, else I developed my first Tizen application in Samsung within a few days of joining.I love to learn new concepts and understanding how they can be used to fit the bigger picture of solving a problem.

When I am not coding, I am generally in the world of thrill and romance through reading various novels or singing on top of my lungs. I love meeting new people and talking. Recently, I have found an affinity for cooking, specially Indian food :) My aim is to write applications and softwares which the user can efficiently interact with and ensure a great user experience! I am available for freelance work too, so if you have any idea which needs a shape, pitch in and do not hesitate to contact me.
