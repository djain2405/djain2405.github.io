About Me

Hi, I am a coding enthusiast, originally from Delhi, India. I worked as a Senior Mobile Developer for Samsung Research India for more than 2 years, where I developed native mobile applications for Samsung Os - Tizen. Thats when I realized I want to create user friendly applications to solve problems and make living easier.

Currently I design and develop Android apps and learning Android every single day. I am Google Certified Associate Android Developer, have small apps currently available in Google play store and actively working with America for Animals for their app Humane Eating Project" as one of the two developers. I also write an Android blog demonstrating solutions to android challenges that anyone might face when learning or working on Android.

When I am not coding, I am generally in the world of thrill and romance through reading various novels or singing on top of my lungs (Checkout my youtube channel :)). I love meeting new people and talking. Recently, I have found an affinity for cooking, specially Indian food :) My aim is to write applications and software which any user can efficiently interact with and ensure a great user experience!
